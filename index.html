The Prisoner's Dilemma
This is a simple ethical game. Imagine you and a random person has been arrested. The police give you a choice, Betray or Remain Silent.
  • If you Betray and the random person Remains Silent you will walk free and they will go to jail for 3 years.
  • If you Remain Silent and the random person Betrays they will walk free and you will go to jail for 3 years.
  • If you both Betray you will both go to jail for 2 years.
  • if you both Remain Silent you will both go to jail for 1 year.
You will play the game twice. The first time the random person will be played by a computer practicing Ethical Egoism. The second game the computer will be practicing The Social Contract Theory.
